About

Design as art
& problem-solving.

For me, design has always been a safe space where creativity and strategy come together. I love storytelling through visuals and paying attention to the little details, and I've worked across print, digital, and branding projects that keep me versatile and adaptable.

Over time, I've learned that good design isn't just about looking artistic, but about being clear, intentional, and expressive. Collaborating with different teams has helped me grow — not just as a designer, but as someone who can listen, communicate, and bring ideas to life in a way that actually works.

My Creative Process

Ideas grow naturally.
Design lands intentionally.

In my creative process, I like to let ideas grow naturally without overcomplicating things. It's about balancing detail and intention until the design feels clear, honest, and true to its purpose.

01
Listen

Understanding the brief deeply — the audience, the feeling, the problem to solve.

02
Research

Immersing in the world of the brief — references, culture, competitors, context.

03
Explore

Sketching, moodboarding, playing — letting the direction reveal itself.

04
Refine

Narrowing in — editing without fear, sharpening without losing soul.

05
Design

Executing with precision — every detail considered, every choice earned.
