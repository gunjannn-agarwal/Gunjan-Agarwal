About

Design as art
& problem-solving.

For me, design has always been a safe space where creativity and strategy come together. I love storytelling through visuals and paying attention to the little details, and I've worked across print, digital, and branding projects that keep me versatile and adaptable.

Over time, I've learned that good design isn't just about looking artistic, but about being clear, intentional, and expressive. Collaborating with different teams has helped me grow — not just as a designer, but as someone who can listen, communicate, and bring ideas to life in a way that actually works.
